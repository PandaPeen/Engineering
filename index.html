Week 10 (10/28/22) Environmental engineering is something I learnt. In summary, engineering is used to solve engineering-related issues that result in waste and pollution in the environment and other locations. Additionally, it may be used to the water filters found in everyone's toilets. In a related project, we had to construct a water filter out of large rocks, small pebbles, sand, and tissue. The water would flow through without a problem in that sequence, from top to bottom. The rocks removed the debris, while the sand and tissue removed the water's impurities. The water seemed drinkable after going through this filter twice.
